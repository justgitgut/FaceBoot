About

FaceBoot

FaceBoot keeps Facebook focused on the feed by removing noisy modules, expanding useful content automatically, and offering optional anti-refresh protection.

Use Settings to control what gets cleaned up. Use Activity to inspect what the extension has removed or expanded during this browser session.

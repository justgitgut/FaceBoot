Tracking since now

Faceberg is a lightweight browser extension that makes Facebook less noisy and more usable. It cleans up your feed, expands truncated content for you, and prevents the page from reloading behind your back.

What it does

  • Feed cleanup — hides Reels, Stories, Sponsored posts, "People you may know", and other clutter so you only see what matters.
  • Auto-expand — opens truncated posts, loads deeper comment threads, and switches to "All comments" automatically.
  • Anti-refresh — stops Facebook from silently reloading the page when you switch tabs, preserving your scroll position.
🌐 Language: Faceberg currently supports English-language Facebook only. If you'd like support for another language or additional functionality, feel free to request it through the extension's store page.

Use Settings to choose what gets cleaned up. Check Activity to see everything Faceberg has done for you.
